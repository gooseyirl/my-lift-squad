## Full Description (4000 chars max)

My Lift Squad is the easiest way to keep track of powerlifters you follow — whether you're a coach watching your athletes, a fan following competitors at a meet, or a lifter keeping tabs on your training partners.

**Create your squads**
Group lifters into squads however you like — by team, by weight class, or however makes sense to you. Add any athlete from the OpenPowerlifting database with a quick search.

**See their stats instantly**
Each athlete card shows their best squat, bench and deadlift, their total, their federation, equipment category and weight class — all pulled automatically from OpenPowerlifting. No manual entry required.

**Favourite your top athletes**
Pin up to 3 athletes as favourites. They appear at the top of the home screen so you can see their stats the moment you open the app — perfect during a live meet.

**Full competition history**
Tap any athlete to see their complete competition history, including every meet they've entered, their results, federation, and placing. Data is cached locally so it loads instantly after the first fetch.

**Backup and restore**
Export your squads and athletes to a JSON file and save it anywhere — Google Drive, your Downloads folder, wherever you like. Restore it any time, even after a reinstall.

**Support the developer**
If you find the app useful, there's an optional one-time in-app purchase to support development. No subscription, no ads, no tracking.

**Privacy first**
My Lift Squad collects no personal data. Everything is stored locally on your device. Competition data comes from OpenPowerlifting, which is publicly available.

---

**Perfect for:**
- Coaches tracking athletes across multiple squads
- Powerlifting fans following competitors at meets
- Athletes keeping tabs on training partners or rivals
- Anyone who finds themselves constantly searching OpenPowerlifting
