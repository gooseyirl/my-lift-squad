# Privacy Policy — My Lift Squad

**Last updated: April 5, 2026**

## Introduction

My Lift Squad ("the app") is built and maintained by Paul Walsh ("I", "me", "the developer"). This privacy policy explains what data the app handles, how it is used, and what your rights are.

By using this app you agree to this policy.

---

## Information I Do Not Collect

I do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, the app does not collect:

- Your name, email address, or any identifying information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage statistics or analytics
- Crash reports
- Cookies or tracking data

---

## Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The app stores the following data **only on your device**, using Android's local Room database and SharedPreferences:

- The squads and athlete lists you create
- Cached competition history fetched from OpenPowerlifting
- Your favourite athletes
- Your purchase status (whether you have supported the developer)
- A rotating quote index

This data never leaves your device except as described below.

---

## Third-Party Data: OpenPowerlifting

The app fetches athlete and competition data from [OpenPowerlifting](https://www.openpowerlifting.org), a publicly available database of powerlifting competition results. When you search for or view an athlete, the app makes a request to the OpenPowerlifting API on your behalf. OpenPowerlifting's own privacy policy governs their data handling.

---

## In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Support Developer") processed entirely by Google Play Billing. I do not receive, store, or process any payment information. Google's privacy policy governs all payment handling. Upon a successful purchase, a flag is stored locally on your device to unlock the purchase benefit.

---

## Backup & Restore

The backup feature exports your squads and athletes as a JSON file saved to a location you choose on your device (e.g. Downloads, Google Drive). This file is entirely under your control. I do not have access to it.

---

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. It contains no features directed at children.

---

## Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, security of that data is governed by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
